üéØ When to Use Calls vs. Puts

Strategic guidance for selecting the right option type

üìà

Bullish Scenarios

Use Call Options When:

  • 1 You expect the stock price to rise significantly
  • 2 You want unlimited profit potential with limited risk
  • 3 You have a positive outlook on the company or sector
  • 4 You want leverage without buying the actual stock
  • 5 You're bullish on market trends or economic indicators
üìâ

Bearish Scenarios

Use Put Options When:

  • 1 You expect the stock price to decline
  • 2 You want to profit from a market downturn
  • 3 You have a negative outlook on the company
  • 4 You want to hedge existing long stock positions
  • 5 You're concerned about market volatility or economic uncertainty
